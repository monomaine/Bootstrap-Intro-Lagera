Announcement

In the latest patch update for Zelda, the team at Nintendo has introduced a few tweaks that will enhance your gameplay experience.

Here's what's new:
• Fixed a glitch causing Link's sword durability to wear out too quickly.
• Increased the durability of certain enemy weapons, making combat more challenging and rewarding.
• Enhanced the graphics and animations of some cut scenes, adding to the overall visual appeal.
• Tweaked the AI of certain enemies, making them slightly easier to defeat.

The team at Nintendo is committed to delivering a quality experience to our loyal Zelda fans. We hope you enjoy the latest patch. (9/20/23)
